Oktie Hassanzadeh

Senior Research Scientist

Oktie Hassanzadeh is a Senior Research Scientist at IBM, working at the intersection of data management, knowledge graphs, and AI. His work focuses on large-scale data integration, semantic data discovery, and enabling large language models to reason over structured enterprise data. He has authored more than 160 publications and is an inventor on more than 30 patent applications, with contributions spanning data lakes, semantic technologies, text-to-SQL evaluation, and knowledge-driven AI systems. His recognitions include Best Paper awards at ESWC, a Best System Demonstration Award at ICAPS, first prize at the ISWC Semantic Web Challenge, membership in the winning team of the FinCausal Shared Task, and two first prizes at the SEMANTiCS Triplification Challenge. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Toronto, where he received the IBM Ph.D. Fellowship and the Yahoo! Key Scientific Challenges Award. He co-organizes the TaDA workshop at VLDB and the SemTab challenge at ISWC, and regularly serves on program committees and review boards for leading venues including VLDB, IJCAI, AAAI, and ISWC.
